Overview

Built a modern, responsive portfolio website showcasing work in machine learning engineering, venture capital, and technology consulting. The site features a custom animated SVG visualization that morphs through three states on a 15-second loop: chemical engineering molecules (benzene ring structure), neural network layers, and VC decision trees - representing the career journey from chemical engineering to ML and VC. The website includes a multi-page architecture with project showcases, Substack blog integration, and contact forms, all optimized for performance, accessibility, and easy deployment.

Design Philosophy

The website was built with a focus on clean, minimal design that prioritizes fast load times, accessibility, and professional presentation. The animated SVG hero section serves as both a visual centerpiece and a narrative device, telling the story of the career transition through interactive visualizations.
